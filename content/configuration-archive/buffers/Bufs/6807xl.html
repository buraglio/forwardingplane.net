Catalyst C6807-xl

Introduced late 2013

The 6807xl chassis is joined at the hip with the Supervisor 6T that was introduced at the same time. The supervisor has its own listing in its place amoung the supervisor heros. The chassis also supports many line cards from the previous supervisor hit list -- as long as they are not too old. All is listed in the data sheet.
