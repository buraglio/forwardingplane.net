Pluribus E68-M

The date on the data sheet is April 15, 2014. This is consistant with other Trident-II switches. The data sheet claims 288,000 MAC addresses while a new Tolly report [30 Aug 2016] makes a distinction between hardware and software hosted MAC forwarding tables. Tolly does not get bored and do reports on 2 year old switches without sponsorship. So Pluribus thinks this is a big thing. Pluribus does not make any buffer claims for this switch, but other products introduced at the same time based on the Intel FM6000 have up to 100 G.
