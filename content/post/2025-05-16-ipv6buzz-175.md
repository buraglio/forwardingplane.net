Returning with more IPv6 basics, we dive deep into that often overlooked and frequently ignored address family - link-local addresses. Link-local addresses are unicast addresses used for addressing on a single link. The intent of link-local addresses is to let devices that may not have a router or global unicast address allocation mechanism still be able to communicate on a network segment.
