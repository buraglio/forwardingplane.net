The NCS5500 family enhanced with 2nd generation fabric cards and fan trays can support Jericho2 line cards. Cisco does not make a fixed configuration switch with the Jericho2. So line cards are us. Cisco has lots of information in the data sheet. Each of these line cards has two Jericho2 ASICs and cooks at about 900 Watts.
