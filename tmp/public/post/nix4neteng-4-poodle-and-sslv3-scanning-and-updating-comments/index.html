NIX4Neteng #4: POODLE and SSLv3, scanning and updating

[Ryan Harden]( “ancker@ancker.net”) - Oct 3, 2014

I whipped up something in bash. It isn’t as automated, but works if you have a list of hostnames/IPs you want to check. #!/bin/sh for i in host1.blah.net host2.blah.net do echo $i echo Q | openssl s_client -connect $i:443 -ssl3 2> /dev/null | grep Protocol done
