Ragile RA-BS6500-32H

Introduced Sept 2020

Data sheet

This BCM56960 Tomahawk switch has 48 50-Gb/s downlink ports. The data sheet does not give the configuration of the downlink ports. The switch is a member of the Open19 standard family. The four downlink connectors are 12-port Molex Impel break-out cable group term tw-pair connectors. These connectors were designed for top-of-rack switches.
