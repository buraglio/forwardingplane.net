Arista 7388X5 Tomahawk 4

Introduced November 2021

Two types of plugin cards are available

  • 8 port DCS-7388-8D QSFP-DD 400G w MACSEC
  • 16 port DCS-7388-16CD QSFP28/QSFP56

    The data sheet at release mis-identifies the card type in the picture above and on page 2 of the datasheet. There are two SFP+ ports on the management card. These ports are connected directly to the CPU and do not touch the ASIC.

    Note that there is no card type with OSPF. Evidence of death of the part?
