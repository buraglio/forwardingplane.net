UfiSpace S9600-32X

Introduced Jul 27, 2021

The introductory press release was followed by more substantial material like a datasheet in December 2021. This uses a Qumran2C ASIC BCM88820.

Of note, port 0 [marked in yellow] can be presented either as a single 100G QSFP28 or split into 4 x 25G SFP28. The switch has 2.4 Tb/s switching capacity which is somewhat less than the sum of all the front panel ports. On the far left side are timing ports (1 pps, 10 MHz, GNSS input) used for PTP.
