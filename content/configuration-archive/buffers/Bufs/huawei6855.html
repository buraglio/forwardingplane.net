Huawei 6855 Trident2+ switches

First mention: April 2016

Huawei has been making trident family switches going back to Trident+ and at least as far at Trident3. These come loaded with Huawei's switch software that looks to be pretty feature-rich. There is no ONIE or white-box feature for switches in this family. The switch data sheet looks pretty extensive.
