AGCX422S and AGCVA48S

Design of these switches is underway but not finished. As such, you cannot buy them. They were announced at the September 2019 Open Compute Project regional summit. Prototypes are being tested with the Jericho-2C which we are told has identical pin out to the planned Qumran-2C. The OCP session that introduced these switches was recorded.

AGCX422S

22*QSFP28 and 4*QSFP-DD

AGCVA48S
