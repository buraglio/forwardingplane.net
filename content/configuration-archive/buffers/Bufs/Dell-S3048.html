Dell S3048-ON

Announced: April 2015

A customer noted in his blog that it is not easy to find info on the ASIC Dell is using in the S3048. So he pried off the heat sink and found BCM56340. His blog is:

http://eoinpk.blogspot.com/2015/08/under-hood-of-dell-s3048-on.html

This switch does not have CPU integrated with the switch ASIC but intead has an Intel ATOM processor. There is a data sheet.
