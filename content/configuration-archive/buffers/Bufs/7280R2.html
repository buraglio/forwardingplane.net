7280R2 Jericho+

Introduced June 2017

Broadcom's Jericho+ ASIC raises the port speeds so that what was 10 Gb/s is now 25 Gb/s. There are twenty-nine fixed configuration varients. Broadly: 

  •  9 models with 60 100 Gig ports 

  • 12 models with 48  25 Gig ports and  6 100 Gig ports

  •  2 models with 30 100 Gig ports

  •  6 models with 36  40 Gig ports and 12 100 Gig ports
    • Jericho+ also has somewhat more route memory. The R2 models also have algo-match which appears to be a feature used to make lawyers happy.

    If 29 models aren't enough, the data sheet covers both the Jericho 7280R and Jericho+ 7280R2 models.
