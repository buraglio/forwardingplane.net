Edgecore AS7326-56X

This Trident-III switch made a trade show floor debut at March 2018 OCP. So far, no data sheet, no press release.

Update September 2018

Edgecore released a full design package to OCP including detailed block diagram. Worthy of note is that the two 10 Gb/s SFP+ ports draw directly off the CPU and are not supported by Trident3 ASIC.

Accepted by OCP as an approved design in October 2018 with a presentation.

Runs PicaOS, Cumulus Linux, ONL, ONIE
