This is a switch fabric device that interconnects AS7926 switches to form very large switch arrays. The data sheet is not a lot of help. For example, the amount of packet memory isn't listed and that this is a cell-based rather than packet-based fabric is not data-sheet worthy.

Arista would put the switches and fabric cards into a chassis where the interconnections are buried inside the box. Edgecore has used QSFP-DD direct connect cables to assemble the composite large switch out of individual boxes.
