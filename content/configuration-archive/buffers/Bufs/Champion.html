Champion-O.N.E switches

Champion-ONE has its roots in selling 3rd party optical modules. They recently have joined the ranks of white box resellers, picking up the Edge-corE line. That the ODM behind the switches is Edge-core is based on reviewing specs and photos of the devices. It has not been announced. Of some interest here is that the data sheets list the software that is supported with each of the switches.

Note that trident2 and trident2+ have different software lists. No trident+ products are listed as the ASIC was obsolete well before Champion began carrying Edgecore switches.
