Edgecore AS7512-54X

A picture of a announcement indicating that it came from Cavium was taken at the OpenCompute Summit in March 2018. So far, no data sheet for this appears on the Edge-core web site.

The text in the picture says: 

Edgecore AS7512-54X
25 GBE DATA CENTER SWITCH BARE-METAL
HARDWARE

The Edgecore AS7512-54X switch is a Top-of-Rack (TOR) or
spine switch for high-performance data centers.

In a compact 1 RU form factor, the switch provides line-rate L2
and L3 switching across the 48 x 25GbE SFP28 with 6 x 
100GbE QSFP28, supporting up to 48 x 10/25 GbE, 6 x 40/100
GbE connections.

The AS7512-54XS can be deployed as a TOR switch support-
ing 10/25 GbE to servers with 40/100 GbE uplinks, or as a
spine switch supporting 40/100 GbE spine interconnects.

The AS7512-54X is a bare-metal switch loaded with the Open
Network Install Environment (ONIE), which supports the instal-
lation of compatible disaggregated Network Operating System
(NOS) software.
