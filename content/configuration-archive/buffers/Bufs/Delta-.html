Delta AGCV208S DCSG

Datasheet timestamp: July 20, 2020

Product Specification [from the TIP Exchange]

Based on Broadcom® StrataDNX™ Qumran-AX, AGCV208S is a dis-aggregated cell site gateway (DCSG) platform fully compliant with carriers’ radio access network requirements.

The scalable and programmable architecture enables operators to deploy in the next-generation network infrastructure.

  • Compact 1RU, hardened design with 295mm in depth
  • Future proof 1GE, 10GE, 25GE and 100GE connectivity
  • A programmable, scalable platform based on Broadcom DNX family with H-QoS and buffering
  • Supports SyncE and 1588v2
  • Supports BroadPTP and Microsemi time synchronization solutions
  • Multiple time synchronization interfaces: 1PPS, 10MHz, GPS, ToD and BITS
  • Front management design to reduce operational efforts
  • AC and DC redundant PSUs
Product Application

Service Providers: Mobile Access Networks and CPE Platform

Features and Benefits

  • Mixed connectivity of 4 x 1GbE BASE-T, 4 x 10GbE SFP+, 8 x 25GbE SFP28 and 2 x 100GbE QSFP28
  • Up to 160Gbps/300Gbps full-duplex switching capacity
  • Supporting SyncE and IEEE 1588v2 with Delta’s API and Library
  • Supporting multiple timing interface of 1PPS I/O, 10MHz I/O, GPS, ToD and BITS
  • Hot-swappable, redundant and, load balancing 1+1 AC/DC power supply units
  • Hot-swappable, 3+1 redundant fan-tray modules
