ufiSpace S9710-76D BCM88850

There is not a lot of info on this switch. Where ufiSpace hangs the datasheet for other switches, the cabinet is bare. On the far left side of the picture, two small connectors are shown that are typically used for IEEE1588 timing. These ports are not mentioned in the feature list. The switch is part of the ufiSpace disaggregated chassis system. It is used with the S9705-48D fabric switch to build systems. Interconnects between the leaf switches and the fabric can be copper or fiber QSFP-DD. AEC (active electrical cable) from Credo can be up to 7 meters long.
